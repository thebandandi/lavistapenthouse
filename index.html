About this space

Experience CABO from the top. Enjoy a unique stay in our exclusive penthouse, featuring a spectacular private rooftop terrace with a palapa, outdoor dining area, grill, refrigerator, comfortable lounge furniture, and best of all: a private Jacuzzi with stunning views of the Arch and the Bay, perfect for relaxing at sunset or under the stars.

The space
Luxury penthouse with private jacuzzi and spectacular views. Located on the third level of an exclusive building, this penthouse offers an unparalleled stay with the perfect balance of comfort, privacy, and style. It features three bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen, a front terrace ideal for enjoying the sunrise, and a rooftop terrace equipped with a private jacuzzi, bar, TV, and lounge area.

Other things to note
We will communicate in advance to coordinate your arrival. Airport transportation available on request. Water or desert activities? We'll recommend the best ones. During your stay, we will be a message or call away.
